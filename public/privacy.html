Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026 · DepositCam by Wade Foundry

DepositCam (“the App”) helps renters document the condition of a rental with timestamped photos and a PDF report. This policy explains what data is used and where it goes.

Data stored on your device

Walkthroughs, photos, captions, notes, and your one-time PDF unlock are saved only on your device (in this browser or app install). We do not host your photos or walkthroughs on our servers.

  • Private / incognito browsing may erase this data when the session ends.
  • Clearing site data or uninstalling the app removes walkthroughs and unlock status.
  • We cannot restore data that was only stored on your device.

Camera and location

The App may ask for camera access to take photos and for location so GPS coordinates can be burned into photos when available. Location is used at capture time on your device and is not sent to us. You can deny location and still use the App; photos will be timestamped without GPS.

Payments

Optional PDF export unlock is processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card number. After payment, the App stores an unlock flag on your device so you can export again without paying twice on that same install.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter for privacy-friendly page and event counts (for example: walkthrough started, export clicked, purchase completed). GoatCounter is designed not to use cookies for tracking and does not build advertising profiles. See their privacy page for details.

Third-party services

  • Hosting: GitHub Pages (static website)
  • Payments: Stripe
  • Analytics: GoatCounter

Those services process data under their own privacy policies.

Children

DepositCam is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact Wade Foundry via the Product Hunt / support channels listed for DepositCam, or email the address published on depositcam.com when available.
